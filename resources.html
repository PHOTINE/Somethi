Software/ Packages - DNA Origami

  1. caDNAno simplifies and enhances the process of designing three-dimensional DNA origami nanostructures -by Shawn Douglas and William Shih at Harvard
    2.
  2. Tiamat Designing DNA Origami Nanostructures - by Sean Williams, Peter Wonka, Stuart Lindsa at ASU.
  3. CanDo Computer-Aided Engineering for DNA Origami - by Mark Bathe at MIT.
  4. DEASALUS Wireframe Scaffolded DNA Origami Sequence Design for 3D 6HB-Based Objects- by Mark Bathe at MIT.
  5. PERDTWireframe Scaffolded DNA Origami Sequence Design for 2D DX-Based Objects- by Mark Bathe at MIT.
  6. METISWireframe Scaffolded DNA Origami Sequence Design for 2D 6HB-Based Objects - by Mark Bathe at MIT.
  7. SAPHIREBayesian Classification of Live-Cell Imaging Datasets - by Mark Bathe at MIT.
  8. FCS Bayes Bayesian Fluorescence Correlation Spectroscopy Analysis- by Mark Bathe at MIT.
  9. MSD Bayes Bayesian Mean-Square Displacement Single-Particle Trajectory Analysis- by Mark Bathe at MIT.
  10. HMM-Bays Bayesian Hidden Markov Model Single-Particle Trajectory Analysis- by Mark Bathe at MIT.
  11. SARSE-DNA Origami - by Kurt Gthelf and Colleagues at Aarhus
  12. NanoEngineerOpen-source Nanotech CAD - by Nanorex.
  13. Mfold RNA and DNA Folding - by Michael Zuker at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute
  14. NUPACK is a growing software suite for the analysis and design of nucleic acid structures, devices, and systems. - by Niles Pierce and Colleagues at Caltech.
  15. Oligo Analyzer - by Integrated DNA Technologies.
  16. Fluorescence SpectraViewer - by Invitrogen
  17. Scadnano is a computational tool for designing synthetic DNA structures, such as DNA origami - by David Doty, Benjamin L Lee, and Tristan Stérin.

Software/ Packages- RNA Origami

  1. NUPACKis a growing software suite for the analysis and design of nucleic acid structures, devices, and systems - by Niles Pierce and Colleagues at Caltech.
  2. Ligateis a Perl script that can merge several individual 3D RNA motifs within a PDB file into a single RNA structure
  3. Traceis a Perl script that can trace a 2D blueprint of a single-stranded RNA origami structure, and output a code that can be used to design sequences in NUPACK
  4. SARSESemi-Automated RNA Sequence Alignment Editor A graphical sequence editor for working with structural alignments of RNA. - by Andersen’s lab

Other Simulators

  1. Automaton simulator can be used to create and test deterministic and nondeterministic finite automata, regular expressions, context-free grammars, and (deterministic) Turing machines. - by David Doty.
  2. Kaemika Chemical Reaction and Protocol Simulator - by Luca Cardelli at Oxford University
  3. CRNSimulator Mathematica Package A Mathematica package for working with networks of coupled chemical reactions. It forms a foundation for syntactic manipulation of chemical reaction networks as Mathematica expressions - by David Soloveichik.
    4.
  4. StableGenis an online tool that allows users to calculate and visualize stable states of chemical systems with user specified inputs and constraints. - by David Soloveichik.
  5. TBNSolver Mathematica package The thermodynamic binding network (TBN) model abstracts the thermodynamics of a chemical system as a tradeoff between maximizing the number of bonds and maximizing the number of separate complexes.
  6. Microfluidic AutomationPurpleDrop is a digital microfluidic device (DMF) for lab automation. - by Douglas Carmean
